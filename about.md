<h1>About This Journal</h1>

<p>This research journal documents daily engineering challenges, deep dives into technical topics, and explorations across mechanical engineering, thermal systems, materials science, and related fields.</p>

<h2>Structure</h2>

<ul>
    <li><strong>Daily Challenges:</strong> Research problems posed each weekday morning with evening follow-ups</li>
    <li><strong>Deep Dives:</strong> Extended explorations of specific technical topics
    <li><strong>Monthly Research:</strong> 4-week literature review cycles on focused topics
    <li><strong>References:</strong> Academic papers, industry reports, and technical resources
</ul>

<h2>Topics Covered</h2>

<ul>
    <li>Thermal/Fluid Systems</li>
    <li>Advanced Materials & Metamaterials</li>
    <li>Structural Mechanics</li>
    <li>Manufacturing & Industry 4.0</li>
    <li>Biomechanics</li>
    <li>Robotics & Mechatronics</li>
    <li>Sustainability & Energy Systems</li>
    <li>Emerging Technologies</li>
</ul>
